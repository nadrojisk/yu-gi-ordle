Yu-Gi-Ordle Filters

You can use attribute filters to better search for cards on Yu-Gi-Ordle!

Syntax

attr:X Show only cards with atr exactly equal to X
attr!X Show only cards with attr of any value BUT X
attr>X Show only cards with attr higher than X
attr<X Show only cards with attr lower than X
